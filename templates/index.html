Designed to empower visually impaired individuals with enhanced safety and independence during their travels.

Key Features:
Obstacle Detection and Safe Pathfinding: utilizes the smartphone's camera to scan the environment in front of the user continually. It identifies obstacles such as walls, steps, or objects obstructing the path and provides real-time audio feedback to guide the user around these obstacles.
Text-to-Audio Message Reader: The application can recognize and convert text from signs, boards, and notices into audio, which is then read aloud to the user. This feature helps users access vital information during their journeys, such as street signs, bus schedules, or store names.
